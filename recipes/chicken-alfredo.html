Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 2-3 pieces of Chicken Tenderloins
  • Fettuccini or Penne Rigate
  • Garlic Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Half a carton of Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Shredded Parmesan
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Cook pasta to preference in boiling water.
  2. Season and fry chicken tenderloins with garlic salt and pepper
  3. Pour half the carton of heavy whipping cream into small skillet at below medium temperature
  4. Add a small slice of butter
  5. After a minute, begin adding garlic salt, a sprinkle of powder, pepper, and shredded parmesan to taste.
  6. Cook mixture until thick and stretchy consistency
  7. Serve pasta, then add alfredo sauce, and top with the fried chicken.
