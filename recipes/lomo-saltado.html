Lomo Saltado

Ingredients

  • 1 or 2 filets of Carne Ranchera
  • 1/2 Red Onion
  • 3-4 Potatoes
  • 1 Roma Tomato
  • Kikko Soy sauce
  • 1 bundle of Cilantro
  • 1 cup of rice

Steps

  1. Wash and set rice to cook.
  2. Cut potatoes into shape of french fries
  3. Fry fries until barely soft.

    Note: This will be important because the fries will cook when we add everything together. Making it too soft at this step will mean it will turn to mush later.

  4. Cut Onion and tomato into thick slices. Pick leaves off cilantro
  5. Trim Carne Ranchera excess fat, then cut into small slices.
  6. Begin cooking meat in pan on medium heat. Add salt and pepper
  7. When meat is barely pink, add fries and soy sauce.
  8. After a few moments add onions and tomatoes.
  9. After a few minutes add picked cilantro.

    Note: The reason you add the cilantro so late is because if you add it too early it will burn.

  10. Serve stir fry mix along with rice and enjoy!
